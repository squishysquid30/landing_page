The blue poison dart frog is a blue frog with black spots on its head and back. They grow to be about 2 inches long and they weigh 0.3 of an ounce. They live in rainforests in Brazil.

The Strawberry Poison Dart Frog is a red frog with blue legs and blue dots going up its back. The strawberry poison dart frog grows to be about an inch long and it weighs 1 ounce. They can be found in the tropical rainforests in Nicaragua, Costa Rica, and Panama.

The mint poison dart frog is a light green frog and sometimes can have darker green legs. There are also some frogs that can be yellow. Mint poison dart frogs grow to be 2 inches long and they usually weigh about 1 ounce. These frogs are found in the tropical rainforests in Colombia.
